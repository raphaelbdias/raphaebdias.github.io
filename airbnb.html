New York AirBnb Case Study

To prepare for the next best steps that Airbnb needs to take as a business, an extensive data analysis was conducted on a dataset consisting of various Airbnb listings in New York.
The objective of the analysis was to identify key insights and trends in regard to host acquisition, customer preferences, pricing, property types, and popular localities in order to increase revenue and improve user experience.
